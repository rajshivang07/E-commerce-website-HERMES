Feature Mobile Phones for One & All

Those not comfortable using a touch screen phone can go for a feature phone. Available in the shape of a bar, feature phones are equipped with keypads and a display screen. These phones are easy to operate and the batteries are quite powerful. The keypad of a feature phone is placed below the display screen. Most of the feature phones being launched in the market have a dual SIM feature, are compact, and do not weigh much at all. Feature phones like any other phone are packed with a bevy of features like an FM radio, Bluetooth connectivity, a music player, an alarm clock, etc.
